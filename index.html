About Kairos AI

Built on the belief that every business has a Kairos moment

In ancient Greek, Kairos means the perfect, opportune moment — the right time to act. We believe every business has a Kairos moment with AI, and most are either missing it or rushing into it unprepared.

Kairos AI was founded in Albany, New York in 2026 to bridge that gap. We work directly with business owners and leadership teams to implement AI that actually solves problems — without the jargon, the hype, or the bloated consulting fees.

We're tool-agnostic. We work with Claude, Microsoft Copilot, ChatGPT, and other leading platforms. Our only loyalty is to results for our clients.
